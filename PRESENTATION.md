## 🛡️ Answering Tough Questions

### "How much will this cost?"
**Answer:** "The prototype is done. Production phase (backend, auth, live data) is €5k-15k one-time development, then €50-200/month for hosting depending on scale. Compare that to a single team offsite at €100+ per person."

### "What if not many people use it?"
**Answer:** "Fair concern. Industry benchmarks for internal gamification tools show 60-80% participation for events like World Cup. We'll also do a soft launch to our pilot offices (Kraków, Munich) to validate before rolling out globally."

### "Is this a priority vs. client work?"
**Answer:** "This *is* about client work indirectly. Engaged employees = better work. Plus, we can reuse this platform for future events—Olympics, Euros, even internal product launches. It's a reusable engagement engine."

### "What happens after the World Cup ends?"
**Answer:** "The platform can be repurposed: predict quarterly results, tech conferences, even intive's own events. The infrastructure is flexible. But the World Cup gives us a perfect launchpad."

### "Security and data privacy?"
**Answer:** "We'll integrate Microsoft Entra ID—no passwords, just SSO. All data stays in intive's Azure environment. We're only storing predictions and points—no sensitive data. GDPR compliant by design."

### "Who will maintain it?"
**Answer:** "Minimal maintenance during the tournament. We'll assign 2-3 people to monitor (rotating shifts during matches). Post-tournament, we can either keep it alive for other events or archive it. Low ongoing commitment."
